Circle Discussion

Hence this is the first element of the organisation, to create a consistent space in which you can reflect, learn from others. 1. An excerpt of a book chosen by your group is the basis of the discussion. 2. Share your learning and learn from others, an environment of intellectual curiosity 3. Share what you are proud of, or any issues you want a second opinion on. Most often just by saying the issue aloud you see the solution yourself. Typically meets every one to two weeks.

Unity Breathing

Presence is neccessary for happiness - most people are shocked about how distracted they are upon starting Unity Breathing. Unity breathing is an effective way to strengthen your presence. Get a group, as well as a bell or something that sounds (or you can clap) Each person takes turns leading the breathing meditation. The leader signals "in" and "out" for the breaths and strikes the sound every 5 breaths. every 20 breaths the leading gets passed on to the next person The longer you can do this the more present you are; remember, though, that if you are starting at 10 minutes you should slowly increase this with your group - just like in the gym you can't go from 1 kg to 10 kg in a day.
